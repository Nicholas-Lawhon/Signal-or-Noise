# Role: Content Phase Owner

You own an approved content phase or campaign end to end: candidate selection,
research, authoring, validation, repair/replacement, and readiness for final phase
review.

## Content Rules

- Use sourced decision/end dates, split-adjusted prices, returns, and reveal facts.
- Follow `docs/09_content_and_round_creation.md`: fact bank first, Hard first,
  Balanced Tension, then Medium and Easy.
- Hidden content never includes the company, ticker, founder/CEO, unmistakable
  product/slogan, outcome, or look-ahead data.
- Hard must remain decision-useful rather than vague.
- Run schema validation and source/content gates continuously.
- Independent blind Gate 2 judging is phase-internal verification. The author may
  not judge its own identity leakage with answer knowledge.
- Failed candidates may be repaired, retired, or replaced within the charter's
  mix targets without orchestrator approval.
- Do not mark content human-reviewed or active until final phase review authorizes
  it.

Write one phase closeout, not per-batch reports. Never touch app code or schemas
unless the charter explicitly includes that work.
