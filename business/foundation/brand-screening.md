## Scope and conclusion

This screen covers the locked product name, reasonable domain variants, and the
handles used by the current channel hypotheses: YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, X,
and Reddit. Public pages and registry responses are untrusted research inputs;
they do not establish rights, infringement, availability for purchase, or
likelihood of confusion.

**Conclusion:** there is no silent blocker in this artifact. Public-brand
selection is explicitly open under D-01 while Signal or Noise? remains the
internal working name. Initial alternatives live in
[name-exploration.md](name-exploration.md). The preferred
exact `.com` is registered, the exact X and Reddit names are occupied, an
Instagram profile exists, and the name is already used by unrelated public
products. The user must choose whether to continue investigating this name and
which alternate domain/handle set to pursue. A qualified trademark review is a
stop condition before public launch, domain/handle registration, or any claim
that the name is clear. See decisions [D-01] and [D-02] in
[legal-support-readiness.md](legal-support-readiness.md).

## Preliminary trademark screening

### USPTO

The official [USPTO trademark search system](https://tmsearch.uspto.gov/search/search-information)
was opened on 2026-07-10 and queried in the Wordmark search for
`"Signal or Noise"`. The visible search set reported **2,378 results** and
returned broad related-word results rather than a defensible exact-match
clearance result. Examples visible in the live result set included:

- **SIGNAL TO NOISE**, live/registered, Serial 77373426, IC 041
  entertainment services, owner CBS Broadcasting Inc.
- **MNI ALL SIGNAL, NO NOISE**, live/registered, Serial 98503447, IC 035/036/038/041,
  owner Hawking, LLC.
- **MORE SIGNAL. LESS NOISE.**, live/pending, Serial 98790027, IC 009/035/041/042/045,
  owner RSA Security LLC.

These are not exact matches and this screen does not assess confusion. They are
enough to require a qualified clearance search covering exact, phonetic, and
related marks in the intended goods/services classes. The USPTO itself directs
users to search similar marks and to consider likelihood of confusion; see its
[search guidance](https://www.uspto.gov/trademarks/search) and
[likelihood-of-confusion guidance](https://www.uspto.gov/trademarks/search/likelihood-of-confusion).

### WIPO

The official [WIPO Global Brand Database](https://www.wipo.int/en/web/global-brand-database)
access point was reviewed on 2026-07-10. WIPO describes coverage of Madrid
international marks and participating national/regional collections, and warns
that national or regional registers may still need separate checking. The
interactive database was blocked in this review environment, so no exact WIPO
result is asserted. That is an explicit research limitation and a legal-review
follow-up under [D-01], not a “clear” result.

## Gate-A follow-up

### Internal and public naming transition

- During exploration, keep the repository name, package scope, database names,
  environment variables, and internal code identifiers unchanged. This avoids a
  technical rename before the public brand is known.
- Once the User selects a cleared name, update current public-facing product
  copy, app/store metadata, domains, social identities, support address, and the
  normative product documents. Because `soul.md` locks the product name, that
  edit requires explicit User approval and a durable project decision.
- Do not rewrite historical closeouts, archived evidence, or old research as if
  the new name existed at the time. Add a concise former-working-name note where
  provenance would otherwise be confusing.
- Renaming the repository, package scope, or database/internal identifiers is a
  separate optional migration. It is not required to launch under a new public
  brand.

1. Keep the exact display name unchanged unless the User approves a product
   decision outside this charter.
2. Before any public use, have qualified counsel repeat exact/similar searches in
   the intended jurisdictions and classes; this artifact is not legal clearance.
3. Have the User choose a primary domain and a distinct social identity after
   that review. Recheck candidates immediately before any registration because
   registry and platform state can change.
4. Do not register, purchase, publish, contact account holders, or create
   external accounts in Growth Phase 1.
